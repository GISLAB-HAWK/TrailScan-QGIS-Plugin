about=<b>TrailScan</b> is a QGIS plugin for mapping of skid trails from Airborne Laser Scanning (ALS) data in forests in Germany.<br><br>
<b>Input:</b> ALS point clouds (.laz or .las) from aircraft, helicopters, or UAVs.<br><br>
<b>Workflow:</b><br>
1. Preprocessing: Convert the point cloud into a normalized 4-band raster (DTM, CHM, MRM, VDI).<br>
2. Inference: Apply the TrailScan model to the normalized raster to generate a probability raster (0–1) named "Trailmap".<br><br>
Download the trained TrailScan model <a href="https://doi.org/10.25625/GEIP6T">here</a>.<br><br>
<b>Output:</b> Georeferenced raster 'Trailmap' ready for further GIS analysis.
