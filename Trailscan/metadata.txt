about=<b>TrailScan</b> is a QGIS plugin for detecting skid trails from Airborne Laser Scanning (ALS) data in Central European forests.<br><br>
<b>Input:</b> ALS point clouds (.laz or .las) from aircraft, helicopters, or UAVs.<br><br>
<b>Workflow:</b><br>
1. Preprocessing: Convert the point cloud into a normalized 4-band raster (DTM, CHM, MRM, VDI).<br>
2. Inference: Apply the TrailScan model to generate a probability raster (0–1).<br><br>
Download the pretrained TrailScan model and example data <a href="https://github.com/GISLAB-HAWK/TrailScan-QGIS-Plugin/releases">here</a>.<br><br>
<b>Output:</b> Georeferenced raster layers ready for further GIS analysis.
